About YooBee

Yoobee School of Design is New Zealand's largest specialist private tertiary provider of creative digital technology courses and related resources.

Established in 1997, we have campuses in Auckland, Wellington and Christchurch which offer certificate and diploma courses, short courses for the community, primary and secondary school students and teachers. We also publish teaching resources for teachers

Until recently, Yoobee School of Design was proudly known as Natcoll Design & Technology. Over the past 15 years Natcoll has maintained its international reputation for producing industry-fit creative professionals. Our graduates have gone on to work in a huge variety of successful creative practices all over the globe – and it all started here. And now, ‘here’ is called Yoobee. All of Natcoll’s renowned heritage, but with a brand new name.
